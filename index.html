Chris is a multidisciplinary designer with a Bachelor of Fine Arts from East Carolina University. He has worked for mission-driven creative agencies, nonprofits, and higher education institutions. Passionate about preserving natural spaces, Chris volunteers time to help with trail restoration, and seeks to make the outdoors inclusive. Chris currently works as a designer for the National Renewable Energy Laboratory supporting research, development, and deployment of renewable energy technologies.
